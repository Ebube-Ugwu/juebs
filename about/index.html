About Juebs Nigeria Limited

over 25 years of Excellence in Automobile services

Core Values

At Juebs, our work is driven by four key values: quality, integrity, innovation and customer satisfaction. We are committed to delivering top-tier automobile services with honesty and transparency, ensuring every vehicle gets the best care. By embracing modern auto technologies like CNG conversion, we stay ahead in providing cost-effective, eco-friendly solutions. Above all, customer satisfaction is our priority -we go an extra mile to ensure reliable, long-lasting results.

Achievements and Milestones

  • 1999 - Juebs was founded
  • 2010 - Expanded into diagnostics and auto check services
  • 2015 - Became an official TATA Motors affiliate
  • 2024 - Launched CNG conversion Services

our history

Founded over 25years ago, Juebs Nigeria Limited has built a reputation for quality, reliability and expertise in the automobile industry. From humble beginings as a small repair shop, we have grown into a trusted name in auto repairs, spare parts retail, diagnostics and custom solutions. Our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction has made us a leading service provider, now proudly affiliated with TATA Motors and at the forefront of CNG conversion technology in Nigeria

our vision

To be the leading automobile service provider in Nigeria, known for innovation, quality and customer satisfaction.

our mission

At Juebs, we are committed to delivering top-quality automobile repairs, diagnostics and CNG conversion services. We ensure every vehicle performs at its best while promoting cost-effective and sustainable mobility solutions.
